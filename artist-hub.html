Show Day, In Six

01

Check-In & Arrival

Have your Artist QR code ready on your phone. Check-in is 1 PM – 5 PM. Our greeting team scans you in and helps you find your section.

02

The Salon Hang

Panels are shared. Work is hung ceiling-to-floor. 3 ft × 3 ft max per piece. We reserve the right to move artwork to make the layout fit or to enforce the rules.

03

Tools & Hardware

Wire or sawtooth hangers are required on the back of every piece. We provide screws and drivers.

Technical Note: Walls are 1/2″ drywall. Do not bring long screws or nails — they won’t hold and they damage the panels.
04

Sales & Connectivity

0% commission. You keep 100% of every sale. No guest Wi-Fi is provided — make sure your POS (Square, Venmo, Cash App, etc.) works on cellular data.

05

Live Painting

Setup begins at check your acceptance email. Drop cloths are mandatory. Bring your own supplies — we provide the space and the crowd.

06

Promotion & Support

Tag @pancakesandbooze to get featured. Our crew is on-site all day to help you find your section, troubleshoot, and get settled.

Hanging Hardware Reference

Wire is preferred for speed. Sawtooth works fine on lighter pieces. Anything else, ask before install day.

Picture WirePreferred
Braided wire across two D-rings or eye-screws. Fastest to install, most stable on our panels.
Sawtooth Hanger
Small metal bracket with a notched edge. Works well on lighter pieces.
D-Rings
Two D-rings, one screw per ring. Solid for medium-weight work.
Salon-Style Hang

Critical Rules & Logistics

The “No Table” Rule

No standard tables or TV trays are allowed in the gallery — they eat wall and floor space, lowering venue capacity. Small stools or low-profile floor bins are permitted, kept beneath your hanging work, for prints and merch.

Assistants

Help is welcome for load-in (check-in window above). Any assistant staying for the show (8 PM+) must have a General Admission ticket — no exceptions on the guest list.

Self-Management

Artists are 100% responsible for their own sales, card readers, and cash bags. No on-site storage is provided for extra inventory. Everything you bring in goes out with you that night.

Tear-Down

All artwork must leave the venue with you immediately after the show. We don’t have venue access after we leave, so if you forget your work, we can’t retrieve it. Neither Pancakes & Booze nor the venue is responsible for left-behind or damaged artwork.
