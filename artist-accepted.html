Wall Space Pricing

Know Before You Pay

01

No Commission on Sales

100% of every sale is yours. Price your work however you want, sell it yourself, keep it all. No gatekeepers, no cut.

02

Two Pieces Minimum, Ten Max

Hang 2–10 pieces, each up to 3 ft × 3 ft. Anything larger won’t fit the walls we build — and crowds your neighbors.

03

The Salon Hang

Your work hangs salon style on 8×4 ft drywall sections we build for the night. Placement is first come, first served — arrive early if you’re picky. Sections may be shared with fellow artists.

04

Tools & Hanging

We provide walls, screws, and screw guns. Busy check-in can mean a short wait for tools — bring your own screw gun to skip it.

No hammers or nails — our walls are too thin for them.
05

Prints, Merch & Title Cards

Sell prints and small merch from a bin under your work at no extra charge — no tables; they eat wall and floor space. Bring your own title cards, index-card size max.

06

Show Night

You’re on the guest list — free entry, but no plus-1s or assistants. The show runs till about midnight. Every piece leaves with you that night — we can’t retrieve left-behind work.
